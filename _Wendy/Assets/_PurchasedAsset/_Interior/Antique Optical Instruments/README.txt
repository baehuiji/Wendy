Asset includes 2 types of models for each optical instrument:
- with transparent lenses (TL);
- with opaque lenses (OL).
Each type has 3 LODs.

All parts of the models are movable relatively its local center.

In "Prefabs" folder you can find ready for use prefabs with LOD Groups.

Demo scene is configured for gamma color space workflow. If you need better quality, you can switch to linear color space workflow.

Below you can see technical specs.

Models with TL has 3 materials (2 for lenses and 1 main).
Models with OL has 2 materials (1 for lenses and 1 main).

PBR textures (2048x2048):
- Albedo;
- Metalness + Smoothness;
- Normal map;
- AO.

Thare are 2 sets of textures for spyglass (with wooden insert and with leather insert).
